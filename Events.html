Please call us at 508-291-2229. We cater to a wide variety of events, from weddings to rehearsal dinners, corporate events, private parties, fundraisers and Holiday Parties. We work hard to make your event a unique experience every time.

We are happy to customize a menu to fit your needs, develop drink specials and other options to make your experience easy to manage while creating a truly unique experience for your guests. We are more than willing to work with wedding planners, florist designers and rearranging the dining room to accommodate whatever the event requires.

* Please be advised that we can only offer cocktail parties after 9 PM, Memorial Day through Labor Day because space is limited. Any party under 20 people can make a standard reservation with a deposit, but reserved seating on deck is not an option. A typical cocktail party is $15 per person plus tax and gratuity and a typical dinner party is $40 plus tax and gratuity. If you choose to bring in your own cake we will charge $1 per person for a cake cutting/cleaning fee.
